How do we determine the significance of genetic variants?

Research Program

We want to be able to accurately predict the phenotype of any and all non-synonymous single nucleotide variants in ion channels involved in the heart contraction cycle. Our approach is to collect as much phenotype information--at high and low resolution--as is feasible for the largest number of variants and then leverage my experimental and computational structural biology background to fill in the details for yet uncharacterized variants.
